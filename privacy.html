Privacy Policy for iOS App "myQRcode"

Thank you for using our iOS App "myQRcode". This privacy policy is intended to give you an overview of how we collect, use, and protect your personal data while you use our app. We take the protection of your data seriously and implement all necessary measures to safeguard your privacy.

1. Collection and Use of Data

1.1. Usage Data

During the operation of our app "myQRcode", we automatically collect information about app usage, including information about visited pages, clicks, search queries, and other interactions. This information is anonymized and used to improve app functionality and user experience.

1.2. Device Information

We collect certain information about the device you use, such as device ID, device type, operating system, and version. This information helps us adapt our app "myQRcode" to different devices and address technical issues.

1.3. Matomo Tracking

To analyze the usage of our app "myQRcode" and collect statistical information, we use the analytics tool Matomo. Matomo is an open-source software that allows us to collect anonymized data about app usage patterns. This tracking helps us improve our app and provide relevant content. No personal data is collected.

2. Data Sharing

2.1. General Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law or you have given explicit consent.

2.2. Matomo Data

The data collected by Matomo is treated confidentially and not shared with third parties. We use this data exclusively to improve our app "myQRcode" and create statistical analyses.

3. Data Storage and Protection

3.1. Data Storage

Your personal data is stored only for as long as necessary for the purpose of data collection. We do not retain your data longer than necessary and take appropriate measures to protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, or disclosure.

3.2. Security Measures

We implement adequate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. These measures include encryption, secure data transmission, and access controls.

4. Your Rights

4.1. Right to Information

You have the right to request information about the personal data we store.

4.2. Correction and Deletion

You have the right to have incorrect or incomplete data corrected or deleted.

4.3. Right to Object

You have the right to object to the processing of your data if we process your data based on legitimate interest.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about the processing of your personal data or if you wish to exercise your rights, you can contact us at dev@marc-hein.de.

Please note that we may update this privacy policy. Therefore, please check our app "myQRcode" or website regularly to ensure that you have the latest version of the privacy policy.
